## Overview

This is the official website of ILITS (Ini Lho ITS) 2022. ILITS is an annual event hosted by Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) to introduce its faculties and showcase student life at ITS.

As a frontend developer for this project, I implemented the information website that introduces ITS faculties and the ILITS event agenda.
